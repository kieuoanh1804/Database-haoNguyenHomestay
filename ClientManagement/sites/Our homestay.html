Our Homestay

Our homestay has been a staple in Phong Nha since before the caves were first found. Our three generations have grown in this homestay and help to make it what it is today. Each generation brings new ideas and energy into the space and we are excited to see how it develops over the next 20 years! We were originally built almost 20 years ago but have updated and rebuilt to suit our travelers' needs several times. Our latest extension was built just 2 years ago and includes a stunning private pool at the base of a tall cliff face. Our modern rooms are tastefully decorated with all the necessities you need to ensure a perfect stay. From Smart TVs, powerful air conditioners, and rain head showers, everything was selected with you in mind. Even with advances to our rooms, our character and purpose has remained the same- to offer a true family experience to all our guests!

We are here for anything our guests may need and are continuously developing our hotel into the modern space it is now. With many tours being offered in the area, we are happy to sit down and discuss your options. Once you have chosen your adventure, sit back and relax until pick up time as we will sort everything out with the tour company.

For arrival or departure, we can assist in booking a bus, train or plane that best suits your needs. We also offer pick up and drop off for all group sizes from Dong Hoi train station or airport to start your journey off right! Let us sort everything out and allow you to enjoy your vacation!
