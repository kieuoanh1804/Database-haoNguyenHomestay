Our room

Our homestay is here to prove that you do not need to sacrifice quality to stay with a local family. Though we are one of the original homestays in Phong Nha, we recently rebuilt our facility to include a modern open plan lobby, swimming pool, and updated rooms. All our rooms are bright and tastefully decorated to ensure that our guests are comfortable. Each room comes with fast WiFi, a filling breakfast, powerful A/C, and a new flat screen smart TV. If there is anything further you may need our family is here to help make your stay as comfortable as possible.
